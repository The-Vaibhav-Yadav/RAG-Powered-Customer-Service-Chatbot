Technical Support for TechTrend Innovations Products

1. Device Setup:
- To set up your TechTrend SmartHub, plug it into a power source, connect to Wi-Fi via the TechTrend app, and follow the in-app instructions.
- Download the TechTrend app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).
- Ensure your device is running firmware version 2.1.3 or higher for optimal performance.

2. Connectivity Issues:
- If your SmartHub fails to connect to Wi-Fi, ensure the network is 2.4 GHz (5 GHz is not supported).
- Reset the device by holding the power button for 10 seconds, then reattempt setup.
- Check for interference from other devices and ensure the router is within 30 feet.

3. Software Troubleshooting:
- If the TechTrend software crashes, ensure you’re using the latest version (check for updates at software.techtrend.com).
- Clear the app cache via Settings > Storage > Clear Cache on your device.
- For persistent issues, uninstall and reinstall the app, then log in again.

4. Account Sync Issues:
- If your account data isn’t syncing, verify your internet connection and ensure you’re logged in with the correct credentials.
- Go to account.techtrend.com and click “Sync Now” to manually trigger synchronization.
- If the issue persists, contact support@techtrend.com with your account ID.

5. Hardware Malfunctions:
- For defective devices, check if your product is under warranty (1 year for most products) at warranty.techtrend.com.
- To initiate a repair, submit a ticket at support.techtrend.com with your order number and a description of the issue.
- Do not attempt to open or repair devices yourself, as this voids the warranty.

6. Software Updates:
- Automatic updates are enabled by default. To manually update, go to Settings > Software Update in the TechTrend app.
- Ensure your device is connected to Wi-Fi and has at least 50% battery before updating.
