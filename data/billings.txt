Billing Information for TechTrend Innovations

1. Payment Methods:
- Payments can be made via credit/debit card, PayPal, or bank transfer through our secure portal at payment.techtrend.com.
- Accepted cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express.
- For bank transfers, use account number 1234567890, routing number 0987654321, and include your invoice ID in the reference.

2. Invoice Details:
- Invoices are issued monthly on the 1st and sent to your registered email address.
- To view or download past invoices, log in to your account at account.techtrend.com and navigate to the Billing History section.
- Late payments incur a 1.5% monthly fee after a 7-day grace period.

3. Subscription Plans:
- Basic Plan: $29.99/month, includes core software features and email support.
- Pro Plan: $59.99/month, includes all Basic features plus priority support and advanced analytics.
- Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing, contact sales@techtrend.com for a quote.
- You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time via account.techtrend.com.

4. Refunds and Cancellations:
- Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for software subscriptions, provided no more than 10% of features have been used.
- To cancel your subscription, go to account.techtrend.com, select Manage Subscription, and follow the prompts.
- Hardware purchases are eligible for returns within 30 days if unopened and in original packaging.

5. Billing Disputes:
- If you believe there’s an error on your invoice, contact billing@techtrend.com within 30 days of the invoice date.
- Provide your invoice ID and a brief description of the issue for faster resolution.

6. Payment Issues:
- If your payment fails, you’ll receive an email notification with instructions to update your payment method.
- Accounts unpaid for 15 days may be temporarily suspended until payment is received.
